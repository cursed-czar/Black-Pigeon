Product



  1. Thermo-electric Charger



    This circuit can come in handy when you are camping in a remote area where there is no electricity available to charge your mobile phone’s or tablet’s battery. If you can make tea or light a fire, you can as well charge your device using this circuit. It uses a simple thermoelectric device.




  2. mini Li-Fi system



    The Li-Fi system consists of normal LED light bulbs and modulating circuits. The idea of Li-Fi is to use existing light infrastructure to transmit data to our computer, Smartphones, tablets etc. This will eliminate some of the main limitations and disadvantages of traditional radio communication, such as Wi-Fi system that we use at home or office.




  3. Home automation kit



    Home automation or domotics is building automation for a home, called a smart home or smart house. A home automation system will control lighting, climate, entertainment systems, and appliances. It may also include home security such as access control and alarm systems.




  4. spud gun



    A spud cannon (sometimes known as a spud gun) is a pipe-based cannon which uses air pressure (pneumatic), or combustion of a flammable gas (acetylene, propane, etc.), to launch projectiles at high speeds.
